--- title: Contact heading: There are a number of ways to reach out to CoEpi. description: Here's where you'll find us on multiple channels. ---

Find us on Github

You can find all of our work open source on Github. Our CoEpi organization holds multiple repositories, including the content of this website. PRs are welcome!

  • CoEpi website

  • CoEpi for iOS

  • CoEpi for Android

  • CoEpi AWS backend

  • CoEpi shared iOS/Rust backend library

Our Contributing guide applies to all of these repositories, and also has information with how you can get added to the CoEpi slack channel if you are interested in being an active contributor.

Find us on Twitter

Feel free to reach out on Twitter - our DMs are open! Dana and Scott both tweet frequently about #CoEpi, and you can also follow the @CoEpiApp handle for news and latest release information.
